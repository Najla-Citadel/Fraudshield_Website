What Happens When You Delete Your Account

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • All personal information (email address, if provided)
  • Account credentials and authentication data
  • App usage history and preferences
  • Device information associated with your account
  • Any stored settings or configurations

Data Retention Exceptions

Note: Some data may be retained for legal and compliance purposes:

  • Transaction logs may be kept for up to 7 years as required by law
  • Security logs related to fraud prevention may be retained for 2 years
  • Anonymous, aggregated analytics data (non-identifiable) may be kept indefinitely

Deletion Process

Follow these steps to delete your account:

  1. Click the "Delete My Account" button below
  2. Confirm your decision in the popup dialog
  3. Your account will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete
